About Me

Currently a software developer with Cotton, Inc., Tatiana Grigorieva was born and raised in Russia where she graduated from Smolensk Economic College with a degree in Accounting. Her career began with the Center of Statistics as a computer operator where her interest and knowledge of computer programming began.

After moving to United States she worked as analyst for West Corp. where she learn the skills of application programming. Later over the years of different employments and volunteer work she was introduced to web development.

Tatiana has been in her current position since March 2015. She is working on maintenance and updates for a suite of software programs and services that leverage USDA High Volume Instrument (HVI) data. She is working on building new features to add to the system which provides significantly improved cotton inventory management and analysis capabilities, as well as electronic communication between producers, ginners, mills, and merchants/co-ops.
